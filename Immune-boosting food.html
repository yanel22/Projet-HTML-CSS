Citrus Fruits

Details: Oranges, grapefruits, lemons, and limes are high in vitamin C, which helps to increase the production of white blood cells that fight infections.

Garlic

Details: Garlic contains a compound called allicin, which has antiviral and antibacterial properties that help to boost the immune system.

Dark Leafy Greens

Details: Dark leafy greens, such as spinach and kale, are rich in vitamins A and C, which help to strengthen the immune system.

Turmeric Rice

Details: Cook rice with turmeric, ginger, and garlic for a flavorful and immune-boosting side dish.
