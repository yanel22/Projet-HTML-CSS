Kefir

Details: Kefir is a fermented drink that's rich in probiotics. It's made by fermenting milk with kefir grains, which are a combination of bacteria and yeast.

Bananas

Details: Bananas are high in fiber and potassium, which can help regulate digestion and promote overall digestive health.

Overnight Oats

Details: Mix 1/2 cup rolled oats, 1/2 cup Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup milk, and 1/2 mashed banana in a jar. Let it sit in the fridge overnight, and top with fresh berries and a drizzle of honey before serving.

Greek Salad

Details: Toss together chopped romaine lettuce, sliced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and kalamata olives. Top with crumbled feta cheese and a drizzle of olive oil and lemon juice.
