Hello

"Welcome to our diabetes-friendly food page!
Here you will find delicious recipes that are low in carbohydrates and sugar,
and rich in nutrients that can help manage blood sugar levels."

Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Skewers

Details: Marinate chicken and a variety of vegetables
(like bell peppers, zucchini, and onions) in olive oil, garlic, and herbs
then grill until tender and serve over brown rice.

Quinoa and Black Bean Salad

Details: Cook quinoa according to package instructions and mix with
black beans, cherry tomatoes, chopped red onion, and a homemade vinaigrette made with olive oil, lime juice, and cumin.

Baked Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

Details: Brush salmon with a mixture of honey and Dijon mustard
then bake in the oven until cooked through.
Serve with a side of roasted asparagus, Brussels sprouts, and sweet potatoes.

Turkey and Vegetable Chili

Details: Brown ground turkey and mix with a variety of vegetables
(like bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes), canned diced tomatoes, and spices like chili powder and cumin.
Simmer until flavors have melded together and serve with a side of whole-grain bread.

Greek Yogurt and Berry Parfait

Details: Layer plain Greek yogurt, mixed berries
and a sprinkle of nuts or granola in a glass or jar for a delicious and satisfying breakfast or snack.
